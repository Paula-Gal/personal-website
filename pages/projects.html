Co-founder - MedX Labs

2025 • Product & Engineering • Side project

A minimal habit tracker with streaks, analytics, and email reminders — designed to make daily consistency feel a bit more fun and a lot less overwhelming.

  • Product design
  • Full-stack
  • Analytics

Problem

Most habit apps felt either too gamified or too bloated. I wanted something that focused on clarity: one view, clear streaks, and simple reminders.

Solution

I built a dashboard where users can see their week at a glance, track streaks, and get gentle nudges when they’re about to break a streak.

My role

I handled everything end-to-end: user interviews, UX/UI design, backend, and deployment.

Highlights

  • Onboarded first 50 users via personal network and feedback calls.
  • Iterated on three different reminder strategies based on user feedback.
  • Improved weekly retention by ~20% after simplifying the dashboard.

  • SaaS
